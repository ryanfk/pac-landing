Kids run.

Open gym, room to move, faces they recognize Sunday after Sunday. The body knowing the parish is the point.

Parents talk.

Coffee, conversation, and the kind of friendships parishes used to be built on. No agenda, no name tags.

Families build it together.

Show up. Bring something if you want. The community is what you make of it — and what we make together.

Bring the family.

PAC is for the whole family at once — not kids in one room and adults in another. About forty kids. Fifteen, twenty families. Once a month.

No registration. No cost. No pre-reading. Just show up after Mass.

All ages. Just show up.
